History of Our Bakery

Minny began over thirty years ago in a quiet farmhouse kitchen right here in Lancaster County. What started as a single indulgent family secret for the perfect chocolate all-butter pie has since grown into a local staple, but has never lost that homemade touch.

We still believe in doing things the old-fashioned way — sourcing our dairy from neighboring farms and baking something fresh every morning. When we traded the original family store for a professional bakery, our mission remained the same: using those treasured Dutch kitchen recipes to make sure every bite tastes exactly like home.
